The Guild

The purpose of the SDG is to promote software craftsmanship amongst its embers. Students will work together to hone their skills while branching out and learning concepts and best practices in varying areas not covered in tradition education. The SDG will fortiy work skills and prepare students for employment after graduation.

The Guild serves as a meeting place for students of all backgrounds and understanding who seek to better understand software components and the development cycle.
